Connect Three

Connect three is a two player game where the goal is to be the first player to connect 3 of your pieces in a row either horizontally vertically or diagonally on the game board

Take turns placing your pieces in the empty spaces, pieces should be placed in the columns and will occupy the lowest empty space in the cloumn

The first player to connect 3 wins, if the board has been completley filled and no player has sucessfully connected 3 then the game is a draw
